Geographical Expansion Plan – AI Career Mentor

Phase 1: Local Deployment

Launch the platform in local colleges and universities to assist students with career guidance.

Phase 2: State-Level Expansion

Expand across educational institutions within the state through partnerships with colleges and training centers.

Phase 3: National Expansion

Promote the platform to students and job seekers across India using online campaigns, placement cells, and educational organizations.

Phase 4: Global Reach

Introduce multilingual support and cloud deployment to make AI Career Mentor accessible to users worldwide. Collaborate with universities and career development organizations to increase adoption.
